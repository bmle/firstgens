The FLIP Lending Library is a space in the Rock containing over 500 free textbooks for students who identify as First-Generation and/or Low-Income to use.

List of textbooks available

Yellow highlighting means the book is currently in the shelves. Blue highlighting means the book is currently in our own storage, and you can contact jonathan_jaramillo@brown.edu or haley_delarosa@brown.edu to request the book. Pink means a book is currently checked out! You can also email us to request a currently checked out book, or get on the waitlist for one.

How to check out textbooks

  • 1. Please record your name, e-mail, book title, and check-out date in the binder provided in the library, so that we may keep track of our inventory.
  • 2. Record the estimated amount of time you need to use the book for; this could be "three weeks" or “the semester” depending on how long you think you need it.

How to return textbooks

  • 1. To return a book, sign the book back into the binder provided in the library.
  • 2. Our shelves are divided into disciplines, and each discipline has its textbooks organized in alphabetical order; please put your book back in its appropriate discipline and alphabetical position.
