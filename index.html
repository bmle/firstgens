Who We Are

  • First-Gens@Brown is a group created after the launch of 1vyG at Brown University. We are not just a student group on campus; rather, we are an entire community.

What We Do

  • Together, we dedicate ourselves to creating a more hospitable, comfortable environment for first generation and low income students on and off campus. The FLIP Library and the "How to Brown" Guidebook are just a few of our projects. If you ever see a community event for first-generation students, you are more than welcomed to join us!
